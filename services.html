Services

The technical expertise and in depth industry knowledge of our professionals makes SsahiPath the ideal choice to assist in building a strong and successful anatomic pathology laboratory in today’s highly competitive and challenging regulatory environment.

Hematology The Hematology department offers an extensive range of diagnostic and clinical services, which cover all blood and coagulation disorders. The expertise and equipment available within the laboratory enables the unit to provide and contract for special projects.

Learn More

Biochemistry The biochemistry unit offers one of the most extensive testing services in the African continent. Analyses of vitamins, hormones, trace metals, lipids, tumor markers, drugs and poisons is provided for local sequesters, and also those further afield. Referrals are accepted from the wider community as well as from health care providers.

Learn More

Microbiology The Microbiology service provides specialist information and testing of microbial pathogens involved in a patient’s infectious disease process and assists with managing outbreaks of communicable diseases.

Learn More

Anatomic Pathology Our methods, training, expertise and knowledge conform to the highest standards expected by our peers and the community at large. We enjoy a close association with various private laboratories and Medical Schools in the United States. We will also participate in Quality Assurance Programs initiated by the American Society of Clinical Pathology.

Learn More

Preventive Medicine The sub-Saharan Africa Healthcare Initiative, in partnership with various local programs, coordinates numerous community outreach programs and public events each year. We are focused on identifying needs and improving our community’s understanding about the importance of early detection and prevention of cancer, as well as new developments in care, diagnosis and treatment. We partner with local agencies to extend our reach to many different populations in our area.

Learn More

Health Screen We understand that disease screening is one of the most basic tools of modern public health and preventive medicine. Our screening programs are designed to control epidemics of infectious diseases and target treatments for chronic diseases. We believe that screening that are conducted in contexts of gender inequality, racial discrimination, sexual taboos, and poverty, shape the attitudes and beliefs of health system and public health decision makers as well as patients, including those who have lost confidence that the health care system will treat them fairly. Thus, if screening programs are poorly conceived, organized, or implemented, they may lead to interventions of questionable merit and enhance the vulnerability of groups and individuals.

Learn More

Diagnostic Imaging Our diagnostic team includes physicians across many medical specialties, including radiologists, pathologists and geneticists. They have expertise in using advanced, minimally invasive diagnostic tests and procedures to diagnose the disease.

Learn More

Digital Pathology Telepathology and whole slide imaging have become integrated into pathology laboratories worldwide. With applications in teaching and research well established, it is now recognized that digital pathology has a role in the clinical realm as well.

Learn More

Biorepository Services Biorepository programs, under the management of Dr. Rebecca Obeng, are essential resources for personalized medicine research, as they enable the study of both health and disease over long periods of time.
