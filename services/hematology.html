Hematology

The Hematology Laboratory provides laboratory tests for the diagnosis and treatment of blood cell and coagulation disorders. We provide services covering the sub-Saharan African region. We are a major referral centre in Africa. The highly skilled local scientists and technical staff work together with a team of local and international hematologists to provide a quality service with in depth clinical interpretation.

The Hematology Department Encompasses

Core Haematology processes full blood counts using high tech Sysmex analyzers.
Blood film morphology is reported with the aid of CellaVision, a digital automated blood cell analysis system. A consultative service is available for referral of blood and bone marrow analysis and a hematologist is available at all times for advice and interpretation.
The Surface Markers section uses flow cytometry technology for the detection and classification of leukemia and lymphoma. Diagnosis and management of many hematological disorders is achieved through a coordinated approach involving morphological, immunophenotypic, cytogenetic and molecular assessment. Cytogenetics offers a comprehensive specialist service to many healthcare providers in the sub-Saharan African region. This team is composed of specialists with an extensive history of relevant research, technology innovation and clinical experience.
The Specialist Hematology and Hemostasis sections receive specimens from around sub-Saharan African region. The Hematology Special Tests section investigates hemoglobinopathies, thalassaemia, and hemolytic anaemia.
Hemostasis provides an extensive range of tests for the investigation of bleeding and thrombotic disorders including clinical trial work.
