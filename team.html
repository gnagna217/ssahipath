The Team

With its combined excellent leadership and profound experience in healthcare and management, the Executive Managment Team is leading the sub-Saharan Africa Healthcare Initiative to become a leading Diagnostics Pathology and Imaging Center in the sub-Saharan region. Our management team features some of the brightest and most experienced professionals in the industry, providing the long-range vision and the strategic thinking that keeps us moving forward. We value a strong corporate culture of innovation and dedication for our communities and believe our experience and strong corporate culture is the key to our success in the past and will continue to contribute to our growth in the future.

Erick Tidiane, PhD., PA, MHA
President / Chief Technology Officer / Co-Founder
As a Co-founder and President of SsahiPath, Dr. Tidiane plays a key role in the planning and operations implementation of the organization’s coordinated care model throughout all SsahiPath care centers. He brings a wealth of knowledge to the team and speaks from an anatomical laboratory professional perspective, which makes him an essential component to the Executive Management Team.
Erick guides SsahiPath in harnessing the power of data, technology, and innovation to create a modern and effective operation that works to improve the health of our communities. SsahiPath initiatives are rooted in three key areas: empower internal innovation, leverage external talent and creativity, and build collaborative communities to tackle cross-cutting issues of strategic importance. As CTO, Erick and his team guide SsahiPath long-term technology strategy to achieve optimal levels of performance and product innovation for our communities.

Dr. Tidiane has an advance Clinical Anatomy Degree from Creighton University, a Master in Healthcare Administration, and Doctorate in Organizational and Industrial Psychology. He is Board Certified in Anatomic Pathology, and his specialties include soft tissue tumors and gastrointestinal Pathology.

Rebecca Obeng, MD, PhD, MPH
Chief Medical Officer
As Chief Medical Officer, Dr. Obeng provides leadership for SsahiPath's highly trained and experienced medical staff, serving as a liaison between the medical and executive teams. Eric Lujan, BS, HT(ASCP)
Sr. Director of Laboratory Operations
As the Director of Laboratory Operations, Eric's role is to ensure the highest possible level of laboratory service quality and operations by:

  • Creating quality assurance indicators to improve laboratory performance through monitoring measurements, outcomes and turnaround time;
  • Monitoring laboratory procedures to ensure accurate and timely reporting while implementing methods to ensure regulatory compliance
  • Ensuring that SsahiPath is in compliance with regulatory and licensing agencies and working with healthcare facilities management to keep licensures valid
  • Providing input into collaborative goals, objectives and strategies for SsahiPath and developing strategies and activities designed to achieve short and long term goals.
As a highly experienced Histology Technician Eric has gained expertise in various laboratory roles including policy development and implementation, establishment of procedural systems and controls, audit and review of guidelines, regulatory compliance and quality assurance. His supervision is exercised over laboratory supervisors, pathologists' assistants, cytotechnologists and support staff . His technical and effective oversight skills and experience include laboratory safety, anatomic and clinical laboratory equipment maintenance, tissue fixation, embedding, microtomy, special stains, immunohistochemistry, digital imaging and laboratory staff training.

Eric Lujan graduated with a Bachelor of Art in Architecture from the University of New Mexico. He completed his Histotechnology Certification Program through Perdue University in Indiana (School of Medicine) and is currently completing a Master of Engineering Degree from the University of New Mexico. Eric is Board Certified with the American Society of Clinical Pathology (ASCP).
